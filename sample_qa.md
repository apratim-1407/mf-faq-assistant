# Sample Queries and Answers

### Q1
What is the lock-in period of ELSS funds?

Answer:
ELSS mutual funds have a mandatory lock-in period of 3 years from the date of investment.

### Q2
How can I download my capital gains statement?

Answer:
Investors can download their capital gains statement from the AMC investor services portal or by requesting a consolidated account statement through CAMS or KFintech.

### Q3
What is the expense ratio of SBI Bluechip Fund?

Answer:
The expense ratio represents the annual fee charged by the fund to manage investments. It is disclosed in the official scheme factsheet and may vary by plan and option.
